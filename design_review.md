# Design Review Report

## Visual Design Feedback
The GUI components developed by gui_coder have a clean and simple design. However, there is room for improvement in terms of visual appeal.
- Consider adding more color and typography variations to make the interface more engaging.
- The button text 'Verify Logs' could be more descriptive and clear about its purpose.

## Usability Feedback
The GUI components are functional and easy to use. However, there are a few areas that require improvement in terms of user experience.
- Consider adding tooltips or hints for users who may not understand the functionality of certain buttons or elements.
- The log verification process could be made more interactive by providing real-time updates on the progress.

## Project Requirements Alignment
The GUI components align well with the project's requirements. However, there is one area that requires attention in terms of alignment.
- Consider adding a feature to allow users to select specific logs for verification instead of verifying all logs at once.
